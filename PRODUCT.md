# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Keepnoto is for people who save links, articles, videos, tools, references, and ideas, then need to return to them with context. Primary users include designers, developers, students, creators, researchers, founders, marketers, and other knowledge workers who collect online material.

## Product Purpose

Keepnoto is a modern link-saving and personal knowledge organization app. It helps users save links with the reason they mattered, organize them without heavy manual work, and later understand what each saved item is, where it came from, and why it is worth revisiting.

## Brand Personality

Calm, warm, intentional. The product should feel like a personal knowledge vault: modern and quietly premium, but simple enough to make saving and returning to links feel easy.

## Anti-references

Keepnoto should not feel like a boring bookmark manager, a noisy dashboard, generic blue SaaS, childish UI, random glassmorphism, or an overloaded productivity tool where users must organize everything before saving.

## Design Principles

- Preserve the reason why something was saved as the highest-value content.
- Make saved links feel useful, valuable, and revisitable.
- Reduce link chaos without adding heavy organization work.
- Use hierarchy, metadata, and spacing to help users scan quickly.
- Prefer calm, reusable product patterns over decorative novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for readable contrast, clear focus states, keyboard-accessible controls, responsive layouts, and reduced-motion-safe interactions. Avoid low-contrast muted text, cramped saved-link cards, and interaction states that depend on color alone.
